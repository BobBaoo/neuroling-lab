title: "NeuroLinguistics Lab"
---

Welcome to the NeuroLinguistics Lab! We investigate the neural mechanisms underlying language processing — from speech perception to sentence comprehension, from first language acquisition to bilingual switching.

Our lab combines **EEG/ERP**, **fMRI**, and **behavioral experiments** to understand how the human brain represents and processes language in real time.
