We are always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our team.

## Open Positions

### Graduate Students (Masters / PhD)

We accept graduate students through the university's annual admission process. Prospective students should have:

- Background in linguistics, psychology, cognitive science, or neuroscience
- Interest in neuroimaging or electrophysiology
- Programming skills (Python, MATLAB, or R) are a plus

### Undergraduate Research Assistants

Undergraduate students interested in gaining research experience are welcome to contact us. Typical commitment: 8-10 hours per week.

### Postdoctoral Researchers

Candidates with a PhD in neurolinguistics, cognitive neuroscience, or related fields are encouraged to email the PI directly with a CV and research statement.
