## Research Directions

### 1. Neural Mechanisms of Sentence Processing

How does the brain parse complex syntactic structures in real time? We use EEG and fMRI to investigate the neural dynamics of syntactic and semantic integration.

### 2. Bilingual Language Control

How do bilingual speakers switch between languages without interference? We study the cognitive control mechanisms underlying code-switching and language selection.

### 3. Neuroplasticity in Language Acquisition

How does the brain reorganize when learning a new language? We track neural changes during second language acquisition using longitudinal designs.

### 4. Neural Markers of Language Disorders

Can we identify early neural signatures of aphasia and specific language impairment? We aim to develop EEG-based biomarkers for clinical applications.

## Methods

- **EEG/ERP** — High temporal resolution tracking of language processing
- **fMRI** — Spatial mapping of language networks
- **Eye-tracking** — Real-time reading and comprehension
- **Behavioral experiments** — Reaction time, accuracy, priming
